Features

Timber Frame Barn

A Maryland classic timber frame barn built using timeless principles, resulting in a sustainable long lasting equestrian structure.


The barn contains four generous stalls; each accessible from both the interior and exterior of the barn. A large hay loft is overhead. Stalls act as run in shelter.


Accessible frost free hydrants are positioned at each end of the barn. The connecting pasture includes a frost free low maintenance waterer. Electric and lighting included.

A six acre pasture is accessible directly from the barn. The pasture is enclosed with a traditional three board oak fencing; a generous gallop surrounds the entire perimeter.

Bank Barn

Beautiful Bank Barn includes two large horse stalls and one small stall, a petite tack room, frost free hydrant, and electric.

A four acre pasture is directly accessible from this barn. A paddock separates the stalls from main field. A full gallop surrounds the entire perimeter.

The Surrounding Property

The property is located in the the desirable Green Spring Valley Hunt club area.

The barns are nestled amongst mature forests. Trail riding is available on/off the property.
